Masanao Hilltop

    Masanao Hilltop is located at Barangay Garcia, San Miguel,Bohol. This place never dissapoints tourist and visitors because of the perfect relaxation it gives. Its a place where life is simple and not letting the stress and happenings in the world get in the way of your happiness. The place is far from the busy streets of the city so you can really feel the relaxing touch of nature in this place. It has this captivating view of the sun as it rises above the skieswith the scenic view above the hill and a breathe of fresh air as you start your day.


      If you wish to travel on your own, drop off at the San Miguel Public Market in Barangay Poblacion.To get there one must charter a "Habal-Habal". Be sure to go there as early as possible to witness the perfect view of sunrise. Always take precautionary measures to prevent unfortunate instances and "Clean as you go".
